Jeff Buckley

Here's a time line of Jeff Buckley's life:

  • 1966 - Born in Anaheim, California, United States
  • 1985 - Moves to Hollywood to attend the Musicians Institute
  • 1990 - Starts playing guitar and singing at cafes in New York
  • 1993 - Begins working on his first album with record producer Andy Wallace
  • 1994 - His album, Grace, is released. In addition to seven original songs, the album included three covers: "Lilac Wine", based on the version by Nina Simone, "Corpus Christi Carol", from Benjamin Britten's A Boy was Born Op.3, and "Hallelujah" by Leonard Cohen.
  • 1995 - Starts touring throughout the USA and Europe.
  • 1996 - Starts working on a second album to be called My Sweetheart the Drunk.
  • 1996 - Dies at the age of 30.
